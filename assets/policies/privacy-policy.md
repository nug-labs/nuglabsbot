# Privacy Policy

This bot stores limited usage data to operate correctly and improve reliability.

## Data We Store
- Telegram user identifiers and optional profile metadata.
- Message handling metadata (request counts, timestamps).
- Broadcast delivery status and analytics events.

## Data Use
- Respond to bot requests.
- Prevent duplicate broadcasts.
- Improve product quality and monitoring.

## Data Sharing
We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by infrastructure providers used to operate this service.

## Contact
Use `/contact` for privacy questions or requests.
